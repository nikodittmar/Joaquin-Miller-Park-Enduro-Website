ABOUT JMP ENDURO

ABOUT THE EVENT

Joaquin Miller Park Enduro was founded with a simple mission: to host exciting mountain bike races that also raise funds for local mountain bike trails and spread responsible trail etiquette. Based in the hills of Oakland, the series showcases the best singletrack mountain bike trails available in the East Bay. The courses include all sorts of terrain; from fast and flowy to steep and technical.

The Joaquin Miller Park Enduro was created by Full Send Mountain Bike Racing, an organization established in the summer of 2021 by a local high school student named Niko Dittmar. The series was kicked off by the Joaquin Miller Park Enduro 2022 presented by Mike’s Bikes, an event that the race host, Niko, had been working tirelessly on. Niko spent an entire year planning, working with local nonprofits, and fighting back against opposition all to ensure that the first Joaquin Miller Park Enduro would be the best event it could possibly be. The event ended up being a massive success, raising $20,000 for local mountain bike trails all while giving the over 100 attendees a great time.

Full Send Mountain Bike Racing intends to head back to the hills of Oakland to host the second Joaquin Miller Park Enduro sometime in the near future. Stay tuned for more details.

MEET THE FOUNDER OF JMP ENDURO

Meet Nikolas “Niko” Dittmar, curently senior at Bishop O’Dowd High School in Oakland California. Niko is an avid mountain biker who races cross country, enduro, and downhill. He started the JMP Enduro as a way of giving back to Joaquin Miller Park, the park and trail system he grew up using, and a place that he cares deeply for.
